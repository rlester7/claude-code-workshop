Claude Code Workshop

You're going to build something cool

Hey there!

Excited to have you at the Claude Code Workshop this week. This isn't your typical "sit and watch" training — you're going to leave with something you actually made.

You're going to make a website without writing any code.

What to Expect

  • Duration: 1 hour, fully hands-on
  • Vibe: Casual and interactive
  • Result: A personalized presentation site you create during the session

What to Bring

Come prepared with a topic for your presentation — something you'd enjoy showing off. You'll also want:

Your Pre-Work Checklist

  • 3-5 images related to your topic (saved on your laptop)
  • A general idea of what you want to present about
  • Your laptop with Terminal access (Mac users, you're all set!)

Need Topic Ideas?

Anything goes! Here are some ideas:

A recent campaign Team introduction Favorite hobby Travel adventures Your pets Side project Book/show you love 2025 goals

The more personal and fun, the better. This is about seeing what's possible, and that's easier when you're excited about the subject.

One Last Thing

Don't worry if you've never used Terminal before. We'll walk through everything together, and there'll be helpers available if you hit any snags.

See you there!
